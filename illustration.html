CIBC REBRANDING ICONS

CIBC is on course to rebrand their business. I illustrated icons for the new brand that will be used on the public site upon launch.

Many icons for the site had already been created so I made sure to follow the same design patterns to illustrate new icons in Adobe Illustrator. The icons have a limited colour palette (red with white or burgundy accents). A border surrounds the solid shapes when for contrast and accessibility.
