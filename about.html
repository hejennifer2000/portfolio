hi! i'm jennifer he.

My parents wanted me to be a doctor. And for a long time, I wanted to be one too. But this was before I understood that being a doctor isn't the only way to help people. That good research and design can have the same impact.

I like design because I can solve problems. Because I can't perform coronary bypass surgery or find the cure to cancer. But why not aspire to create the tools that empower those who can?
